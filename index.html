Frequently Asked Questions

What is the exam format?

The Medha Scholarship Test consists of multiple-choice and subjective questions testing students' knowledge in core subjects.

What are the scholarship benefits?

Top performers can receive scholarships covering 100%, 50%, or 25% of tuition and transport fees.

When will the results be announced?

The results will be announced on April 1st. Notifications will be sent via email and SMS.

How do I prepare for the scholarship test?

Focus on your Class X syllabus and fundamental concepts. A detailed syllabus is available in the downloadable brochure.
